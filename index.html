What she's about

Tama's dedicated to making the world a better place. She wants to solve the world's problems. She's started two non-profits. One of them, the social impact project is dedicated to supplying under-priviledged school children with school supplies. The other, The Student project, it's getting off of the ground, is dedicated to being a platform through which those kept out of the mainstream education pipeline can acquire employable skills
Tama believes that equal opportunity should be granted to everyone regardless of their gender. She's outspoken on issues of gender-based violence and sexism Tama represents the future of the African continent
